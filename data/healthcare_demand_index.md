# Healthcare Demand Index Dataset

The Healthcare Demand Index is the primary analytical dataset
generated by the Health Informatics Hub platform.

This dataset estimates healthcare demand by geographic region.

The index combines population demographics, healthcare infrastructure,
and disease burden indicators.

---

# Dataset Fields

| Field | Description |
|------|-------------|
country_id | country identifier |
region_id | geographic region |
year | calendar year |
population | total population |
population_age_65_plus | elderly population |
hospital_beds | total hospital beds |
beds_per_1000 | hospital beds per 1000 population |
disease_burden_index | estimated disease burden |
insurance_coverage_rate | insured population percentage |
demand_score | calculated healthcare demand score |
demand_category | Low / Medium / High |

# Purpose

The Healthcare Demand Index enables:

• healthcare infrastructure planning  
• hospital investment analysis  
• population health forecasting  
• healthcare resource allocation
