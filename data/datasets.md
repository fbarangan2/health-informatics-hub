# Data Sources

Health Informatics Hub is designed as a country-agnostic healthcare analytics platform.

The system ingests demographic, healthcare utilization, and infrastructure data
from multiple countries to support healthcare planning and hospital demand forecasting.

## Data Categories

### Population Demographics
Examples:
- population by age group
- population projections
- household population
- geographic distribution

### Healthcare Infrastructure
Examples:
- hospitals
- clinic networks
- bed capacity
- healthcare workforce

### Healthcare Utilization
Examples:
- insurance claims
- procedure volume
- outpatient visits
- inpatient admissions

### Disease Burden
Examples:
- chronic disease prevalence
- mortality rates
- epidemiological indicators

## Initial Implementation Dataset

The first implementation will use demographic datasets from the Philippines
to build and validate the platform architecture.

Source:
Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA)

Future datasets may include sources from other countries such as:

- United States healthcare datasets
- international public health data
- government healthcare statistics
